# Tooldocker Ecommerce Blueprint (India-Focused)

## 🧠 Product Vision

Tooldocker is a **simple, powerful, admin-controlled ecommerce platform** designed for the **Indian market**, targeting:

- Contractors
- B2B buyers
- Real estate developers
- Industrial tool buyers

The platform focuses on **fast product discovery, reliable purchasing, and high-quality listings**, all controlled by Admin.

⚠️ Vendor system is NOT included in Phase 1 (can be added later).

---

## 🎯 Core Business Model

- Admin manages all products
- Buyers purchase directly from platform
- Revenue comes from product sales
- Future scope: vendor onboarding

---

## 👥 User Roles

### 1. Admin (Primary)
- Add/edit/delete products
- Bulk upload products
- Manage categories
- Manage orders
- Control pricing & inventory
- Use AI for product creation

### 2. Buyer (Target Users)
- Contractors
- Builders
- Industrial buyers

Capabilities:
- Search products
- Browse categories
- Add to cart
- Checkout
- Track orders

---
